Welcome Survivor

The year is 2023, it has been 10 years since the infection (Cordyceps Brain Infection) started spreading. It originated in South America and spread to humans through infected crops. Unfortunately, 60% of humanity died or was infected by CBI only several months after the initial spread. Once infected, the fungus grows in humans while they are still alive. The host will then go through four stages of infection, effectively turning its hosts into a zombie like state until it eventually kills them.

There is no cure, there is no preventive measure humans can take to stop it.

Within the first year "quarantine zones" were established in order to keep the remaining population safe, although there is often the spread of infection and internal uprises.
