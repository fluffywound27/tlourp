LAW & ORDER

No society can exist without rules and laws in place. Chernogorsk's Quarantine Zone is no exception! Please make sure to acquantince yourself with all of them. Ignorance is no excuse! All of our rules and laws can also be found in the communities Discord. On the server-rules and qz-laws.

There's a 3 strike system implemented:

  • 1st Strick = 24hr timeout
  • 2nd Strike = week timeout
  • 3rd Strike = Ban

    • All ban/timeouts are decided by the mod team and we aim to keep punishments as fair as possible.

    If you witness anyone being unpleasant towards other members, please report it via support tickets, and if you find someone is breaking server rules you are required to report it to an admin.

    We have assigned warning levels individually to rules so everyone can clearly see the punishments for specific rule breaks.

    Rules with “RP ONLY” will need to be clipped or assessed by admins before punishments are dealt. “RP ONLY” rules must start with a roleplay interaction. Failure to roleplay before engaging in conflict will result in punishment.

    ROLEPLAY INTERACTIONS

    Members must participate in a roleplay interaction or provide warning shots before initiating combat outside of the QZ. If you are killed without warning and did not partake in a roleplay interaction we suggest members to clip footage and send it to admins. Killing & robbing is not permitted inside the QZ.

      Civilians must roleplay a robbery/hold up situation before shooting.
      If a target is out of range and you decide to shoot, you must provide a warning shot.
      Criminals/robbers must talk & tell the victim they are being robbed before shooting.
      Criminals must be within 20 metres of their victim before starting a roleplay interaction.

    WARNING SHOTS:

      Warning shots must be unsuppressed.
      A minimum of 1 warning shot must be fired.
      Warning shots must be fired in the direction of the target so they can hear the bullet snap.
      Warning shots must not hit the target.

    DETAINMENT RULES:

      Criminals only have 1 escape attempt.
        If a criminal fails to escape you must comply with your arrest for roleplay purposes.
        Members must not Respawn if knocked out, they must comply when being arrested If disconnected please return to area at which you disconnected.
        If you kill yourself while in the detainment your character is still wanted.
        Prisoners must not kill themselves while in custody.

      ANTI ESPIONAGE RULES:

        Spying and espionage is not allowed.
        Members are not to share base codes with other factions whilsts leaving or joining a faction.
        Members may NOT join a faction for leverage and gain.

      An auto-ban feature has been enabled over the QZ. Killing inside of it will get you automatically banned. Please keep killing/robbing/raiding outside of the QZ to avoid potential punishments.
